Frequently Asked Questions

PixelMath is on a mission to make math as fun and addictive as games. We do this through our PixelMath App where students have a gameful experience while solving thousands of questions. We also run live online classes for a limited cohort of students.

PixelMath International Math Challenge is an annual competition where students from all over the world participate in a fun and engaging math competition in a series of 6 contests over the year.

6 Qualifying contests will be held and the final contest will be held on National Math Day on 22nd December 2022. Top 100 students from all 6 contests will qualify for the final event.

Scores are calculated on the basis of accuracy percentage and time taken to solve a question.

The syllabus will be according to the grade the student is in, the syllabus will be different for all the contests- you can download the syllabus PDF from the links above.

Students from Grade 3 to Grade 10 can participate in the contest.
